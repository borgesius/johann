# HFT Firm Game Stage 4: Incidents And Polish

Take the HFT firm game from a promising simulation to a robust, digestible prototype with failure modes, progression, and onboarding.

This stage should preserve earlier systems and make the game feel like a complete management loop rather than a pile of subsystems.

## Goal

Add:
- incidents, risk, or near-fiasco style events driven by management decisions
- progression and economy tuning that gives the player a reason to care about decisions
- onboarding or guidance so the early game is approachable
- logs, alerts, or history views that help the player understand what happened
- consequences from a cutthroat performance culture, including perverse incentives and political fallout

## Required Outcomes

- bad decisions should create plausible incidents, not arbitrary punishment
- the player should have a readable path from “I am learning the systems” to “I am composing them cleverly”
- the UI should help the player recover from or understand mistakes
- the game should feel more complete, not merely more complicated

## Delivery Expectations

Preserve the repo quality while adding:
- incident/risk systems
- progression/economy systems
- onboarding/help/tutorial affordances
- docs for balance and incidents
- tests or smoke coverage
